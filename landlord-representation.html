Expert strategies with proven results

KMC Savills' project marketing team cooperates with property developers to effectively launch and market real estate developments. The team is capable of handling the project's positioning, creating marketing strategies and tools, taking the lead in leasing or sale negotiations, and providing other post-lease or sale services. With the company's assistance, developers can fully concentrate on implementing the project and their vision.

The KMC Savills Advantage

KMC employs different forms of market strategies that involve using its established network of contacts: trusted clients, other highly recognized companies, and affiliates. The team can market projects through exposure in various media platforms involving press releases and advertisements, effectively spreading brand and project awareness among the target market. It is also capable of producing and disseminating both electronic and print brochures to potential buyers as well as pertinent members of the real estate and media industries.

A Broad range of project marketing services

From media exposure down to client presentation, KMC Savills can assist developers in finding the audience and targeting the market for any type of mixed-use, commercial, or residential property. The company will provide effective real estate marketing tools to ensure the clients' success.

Who we work with:

  • Property owners and developers that need assistance with the marketing of their properties; either for leasing or selling
  • Project marketing of residential complexes for sale

What we offer:

  • Leverage on existing database of 40,000+ for targeted campaigns
  • Strategic above and below the line campaign planning
  • Marketing collateral design and production facilitation
  • Powerpoint and multimedia presentations
  • Websites and pages
  • Inspections
